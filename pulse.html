Pulse

Created with Dhanya Mahesh and Reva Kulkarni

Overview



Ever been so immersed in your music that you forget about reality? We have. That's why we developed Pulse Headphones, a revolutionary new technology that notifies users when their name has been called. Our tool can be further applied as an alarm, safety alert, or hearing aid.

How It Works



First, audio is picked up through the microphone and sent to Arduino. Arduino sends the audio to BitVoicer software. BitVoicer analyzes this data and sends strings to Arduino. Arduino then analyzes strings for user's name, and if found, sends electrical signals to buzzers.

Affordability: Pulse Headphones are $30, at least 1/5 the price of comparable products by Bose, Beats, and SkullCandy
